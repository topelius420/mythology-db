## Database Structure

### Each Entry Should Include:
1. **Name** - deity/creature/concept
2. **Origin** - mythology/culture
3. **Domain** - what they represent
4. **Key Stories** - major myths
5. **Symbols/Attributes**
6. **AI Analogy** - how it maps to digital concepts
7. **Modern Relevance**
8. **Sources** - DuckDuckGo/Tavily citations
